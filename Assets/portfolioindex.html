About

I am a young, motivated, hardworking prospective graduate of Eleven Fifty Academy's 12 week .NET program. Before Eleven Fifty, I was working at a small rural hospital's Emergency Department as an Emergency Medical Technician. Growing up I always saw myself working in the medical field and also had a love for sports and competition from a young age. This led me down the path of receiving a Bachelor of Science in Exercise Science from Indiana State University, and eventually into patient care and emergency medicine. Although I enjoyed the fast-paced environment of the hospital and medical profession, I found myself wanting something more intellectually stimulating and challenging. I was introduced to the world of coding through a family member and after some research, decided it was the right path for me to pursue. I am excited to launch this new career and professional adventure. Thank you for visiting my website and taking the time to view my work.
